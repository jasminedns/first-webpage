Welcome to Animes!

What a pleasure seeing you here! If you're looking for suggestions, advices or to simply get inspired for your next trip, you're may be in the right place!

Hi! I’m Jasmine, a 26-year-old from Italy (though not for long!). You might be wondering why I started a travel blog. Well, I say, why not? My passion for travel comes from my parents. Their love story began when my dad traveled to Brazil and met my mom. They took me and my siblings on many adventures, sparking my love for exploring new places. I even trained to become a flight attendant, but I left that path for personal reasons and never tried again. At 21, I made the promise to myself to visit at least 30 countries before turning 30, and I’ve already achieved that goal! I hope you find some inspiration here!
